Your Private Consultation

A comprehensive approach to your skin and hair care needs

Why Consultation is Essential

A full consultation with Dr. Matiur Rahman Chowdhury is essential prior to any treatments taking place.

For an effective treatment plan to deliver the best possible results, we ensure that we fully understand the condition that needs to be treated. Sometimes there are underlying factors which must be fully appreciated and treated in order to get a good outcome for the patient.

What to Expect During Your Consultation

✔ Detailed Discussion: A comprehensive conversation to fully understand your skin or hair concerns and expectations.

✔ Medical & Treatment History: Review of relevant medical background and any previous treatments undertaken.

✔ Lifestyle Assessment: Evaluation of daily routine including work, stress, diet, hydration, and relaxation habits.

✔ Treatment Plan & Quotation: Clear explanation of suitable treatment options along with pricing and next steps.

Personalized Treatment Planning

Based on a full understanding of a person's physiology as well as their hopes for treatment, a suitable plan can be suggested and prices would be confirmed. Depending on the time available and type of treatment required, it may be possible to start right away in the same session. If not, then a treatment schedule and appointments would be discussed.
