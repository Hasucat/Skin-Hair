Lip Enhancement

Volumising Lip Fillers for Natural-Looking Results

Volumising Lip Fillers

Lip enhancement is a non-surgical procedure which aims to enhance the size and shape of the lips.

This is achieved through the injection of dermal fillers to achieve fuller, plumper lips which look natural.

Lip enhancement is ideal for people who feel that their lips aren't full, plump or youthful enough. The use of the latest lip fillers can remove wrinkles and creases from the patient's lips, although the procedure is just as effective on younger women who believe their pout could use a boost.

How lip enhancement works

The filler involved in lip enhancement procedures is called hyaluronic acid (HA). A clear gel, HA is a naturally-occurring substance found in the body and, like collagen, it plays an essential role in ensuring that our lips stay full and plump. HA is injected into the lips in very small amounts using a fine needle, which fills out the lips and removes any wrinkles that may be there.

Lip enhancement FAQ

Will it hurt?

Many modern lip fillers contain a local anaesthetic making the experience quite comfortable. In addition, local anaesthetic gel and/or ice may be used for further comfort.

All treatments at our clinic are made as comfortable as possible according to the treatment and the individual's personal requirements. We use topical anaesthetic cream. If necessary, a dental block anaesthetic may also be used.

Are there any negative side-effects to lip enhancement?

You may experience pain and swelling around the lips for a few days after the procedure, though this should subside naturally.

How long will the results last for?

The results of a lip enhancement procedure can be expected to last between 6-9 months, after which the treatment can be repeated.

How long will the treatment take?

Lip enhancement can take up to half an hour, depending on the number of areas that require attention.

How can you ensure a good result?

After injecting lip filler, our professionals spend time ensuring that it is perfectly distributed. This gives a smooth and even finish.

Common Questions

Is lip enhancement safe?

Yes, when performed by a qualified and experienced practitioner like Dr. Matiur Rahman Chowdhury, lip enhancement is a safe procedure. We use only high-quality, medically-approved fillers.

Can I go back to work immediately?

Most patients can return to normal activities immediately after treatment. Some minor swelling or redness may occur but typically resolves within a few hours to a day.

Most patients achieve their desired results in a single session. Maintenance treatments are recommended every 6-9 months to maintain optimal results.
