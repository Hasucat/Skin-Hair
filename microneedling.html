Micro-Needling Skin Therapy

Dermaroller® & Dermapen Treatments for Skin Rejuvenation

Micro-Needling Skin Therapy

Dermaroller® therapy is a treatment which rejuvenates the skin and repairs scars with the use of tiny micro-needles.

Dermaroller® therapy can be used to treat many cosmetic blemishes, ranging from acne scars to wrinkles, and is a highly-effective non-surgical method which pushes the skin to heal and repair itself.

How does it work?

Dermaroller® therapy involves the use of a Dermaroller® medical device, which is a 'roller' with hundreds of tiny micro-needles. This creates tiny pin-prick holes in the skin, which will heal within a day. These encourage the skin to repair and regenerate itself by producing more collagen and elastin – the substances which help to keep our skin flexible and strong – and the skin will continue to heal and grow for months after the treatment.

Who is Dermaroller® therapy for?

Dermaroller® therapy is used in the treatment of a wide range of skin conditions, including acne scars, lines and wrinkles, sun-damaged skin and stretch marks.

For this reason, we see a wide range of patients interested in Dermaroller® therapy – it's for anybody who feels that the aforementioned problems are causing them to feel insecure or unhappy and who is seeking a treatment that will have lasting positive effects.

The Dermapen

The Dermapen is a small microneedling tool which is used for very precise treatment of skin problems.

The Dermapen device contains a number of tiny 'micro needles' which penetrate the skin's outer layers. This stimulates the skin's own healing and rejuvenation processes and also allows serums or products to access deeper layers of the skin than would normally be achieved.

Dermapen treatment can be used on its own or alongside other microneedling treatments.

Dermaroller vs Dermapen

  • Dermaroller is the best known. This roller device enables the practitioner to treat a larger area quickly and evenly for generalised skin stimulation treatment. Dermaroller comes in a variety of sizes – smaller rollers are suitable for the face/cheeks/neck; while larger devices can be used for the body areas such as abdomen
  • Dermapen is an automated microneedling device, but can achieve the best results through targeting specific problem areas, like around the eyes, specific lines, scarring, acne scarring

All these treatments are available at "Skin&Hair By Dr. Matiur", Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Dermaroller® FAQ

Is it safe?

Yes, completely. The devices themselves are for single-use, so every device is clean and safe, and it has become an established and respected cosmetic treatment in recent years.

Will it hurt?

Any discomfort that is experienced will be minimal. We use a local anaesthetic to numb the skin around the treated area before the Dermaroller® therapy begins.

When will I see the results?

While the skin will continue to heal and grow for months after your treatment, you can expect to see firm results around six weeks after undergoing Dermaroller® therapy.

Dermapen FAQ

What problems can Dermapen treat?

  • Acne Scarring
  • Pigmentation
  • Large pores
  • Lines and wrinkles
  • Dull skin

Is the Dermapen safe?

Yes, it's very safe. This is a non-invasive treatment which involves no injections, lasers or heat. The Dermapen instrument is sterile and our clinic has very high standards of hygiene to minimise any risk of infection.

Does it look natural?

Results of Dermapen are very natural indeed. The treatment works in harmony with the body's own processes, stimulating healing, repair and cell renewal. The results is that the skin refreshes itself, so there is no danger of looking 'overdone' or fake at all

What product is applied to the skin after treatment?

This depends on the particular issue being treated. Options include:

  • Hyaluronic acid for hydration/subtle volume
  • Serum
  • PRP ( Platelet Rich Plasma )
  • Specific Vitamins

What should I expect after treatment?

  • The skin will appear red/flushed for 1-2 days after treatment. This shows that it is responding to the treatment and shows the start of the healing/renewal process
  • Wear a high factor mineral-based sun protection to protect your skin. This is advisable year-round to prevent ageing, but essential for 2-4 weeks after treatment while the skin is healing
  • Avoid any active/stimulating skin preparations for a couple of days (i.e. AHA's, retinol (vitamin A) or anything acidic)
  • A good and suitable daily skincare regime is crucial to help promote and achieve optimum results. We always advise on this.

When will I see results?

  • You will see a plumping effect almost immediately
  • You will notice a fresher skin within 6 weeks
  • Skin will continue to improve over 6-12mths
