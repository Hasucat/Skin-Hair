About Our Clinic

"Skin&Hair By Dr Matiur" has been established as a unique concept in integrated skin and hair care and treatment. Bringing patients the latest skincare technology and techniques, we have only expert and experienced doctors who are highly skilled in the latest skin and hair treatments.

We offer all types of clinical – dermato surgical and aesthetic procedures like chemical peels, all types of laser treatments, PRP / GFC / Exosome / PDRN, anti-ageing, wrinkle-relaxing injections (BotA), dermal fillers, collagen stimulating PDO – PLLA - PCL threads, mesotherapy, face and body contouring and some exclusive combination treatments.

Dr Matiur Rahman Chowdhury is the most experienced and highly skilled Clinical - Aesthetic Dermatologist of Bangladesh. He is one of those very few doctors who started concept of Aesthetic Dermatology in Bangladesh.
